### Q87
*   **Your Answer**: **As** there are no opportunities..., **so** it's hard for them...
*   **Correction**: **As** there are no opportunities..., it's hard for them... (OR: There are no opportunities..., **so** it's hard...)
*   **Analysis**:
    *   `As... so...` -> ❌ **Critical Grammar Error**. In English, you cannot use "Because/As" and "So" in the same sentence. Choose one.

### Q89
*   **Your Answer**: ...now it's **hard task** to run a business
*   **Correction**: ...now it's **a daunting/hard task** to run a business.
*   **Analysis**:
    *   `hard task` -> Countable noun singular. Needs article **a**.
    *   **Better Word**: `Daunting task` (Hint) is much more native than `hard task`.

### Q90
*   **Your Answer**: ...because of the **mechanism**
*   **Correction**: ...because of **mechanisation**.
*   **Analysis**:
    *   `mechanism` (机制/机械装置) -> ❌ Wrong word.
    *   `mechanisation` (机械化) -> ✅ Correct noun.

### Q93
*   **Your Answer**: ...linke between advertisements... and the **comman fat people**
*   **Correction**: ...**link** between ... and **widespread obesity**.
*   **Analysis**:
    *   `linke` -> ❌ **Spelling**.
    *   `comman fat people` -> ❌ **Too Informal/Rude**. "Fat people" is rarely used in academic writing. Use **Obesity** (肥胖症) or **Overweight population**. "Common" should be **Widespread** or **Prevalent**.

### Q95
*   **Your Answer**: ...buy something they don't need, **which should be exercised tight control**
*   **Correction**: ..., **which should be strictly regulated** (or: ..., **control over which should be tightened**).
*   **Analysis**:
    *   `which should be exercised tight control` -> ❌ **Grammar**. "Strict control" is the object of "exercise", not the subject. Passive voice here is clumsy. Use the verb **Regulate**.

### Q96
*   **Your Answer**: This can **attribute** to **air condition**, thus reducing... **breathal illness**
*   **Correction**: This can **contribute** to **better air quality**, thus reducing... **respiratory diseases**.
*   **Analysis**:
    *   `attribute to` (归因于) -> ❌ Context means "help to improve". Use **Contribute to**.
    *   `air condition` (空调?) -> ❌ You mean **Air Quality**.
    *   `breathal` -> ❌ **Non-existent word**. Use **Respiratory** (呼吸道的).

### Q97
*   **Your Answer**: **sensationlising** social problems
*   **Correction**: **sensationalising** social problems.
*   **Analysis**:
    *   `sensationlising` -> ❌ **Spelling**.

## 📚 Critical Mistakes to Memorize (错题本)

1.  **[Spelling]**:
    *   **New** (not *newe*)
    *   **Link** (not *linke*)
    *   **Customers** (not *costomers*)
    *   **Pollution** (not *pollutation*)
    *   **Sensationalising**
2.  **[Grammar - Chinglish]**:
    *   **As..., so...** (NEVER use together. Drop 'so'.)
3.  **[Vocabulary]**:
    *   **Obesity** (Not *fat people*)
    *   **Respiratory diseases** (Not *breathal illness*)
    *   **The environment** (Don't forget 'the')
    *   **Mechanisation** (Not *mechanism*)
