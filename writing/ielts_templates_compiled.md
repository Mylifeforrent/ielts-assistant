这份文档整理了您提供的写作模版，并进行了**优化**。
> ⚠️ **注意**: 原模版中出现的 "Nowadays, it is a noticeable phenomenon that..." 等句型属于典型的"模版句"，容易被考官压分。我已将其替换为更自然、高分的表达。

## 🗺️ Task 1: Maps / 地图题

**Introduction (开头段):**
> The two maps **illustrate** the developments that have taken place in a particular place called [Place Name] over a ten-year period from [Year] to [Year].

**Overview (综述段 - 关键句):**
> **Overall, it is evident that** the area has become more **urbanized** (or commercialized) than before. The most noticeable changes are the construction of new infrastructure and amenities, often at the expense of green areas.
> *（注：原模版 "congested" 带有负面色彩，"modernized/urbanized" 更中性准确。）*

**Body Paragraphs (主体段):**
> **To begin with,** in [Year], [Feature A] was located in the [Direction]. **However,** it was replaced by [Feature B] to make way for [New Purpose].
>
> **Furthermore**, the [Building] has been **enlarged** by adding [Extension], which drastically reduced the size of [Other Area].
>
> **Finally,** it is worth noting that only [Feature C] and [Feature D] have remained **largely unchanged** over the period, although [Feature D] was slightly renovated.

### 🧠 记忆口诀

> 🇨🇳 **illustrate 开场白，overall 说大改，这拆那建讲变化，unchanged 结尾最自在。**
>
> 🇬🇧 *Illustrate to start the show, Overall tells how things grow, Replaced and enlarged in detail, Unchanged ends the map's tale.*

## ⚙️ Task 1: Process / 流程图

**Introduction (开头段):**
> The diagram **delineates** the process of producing [Product] / the life cycle of [Species].

**Overview (综述段):**
> **Overall,** the process consists of [Number] distinct stages, **commencing with** [Step 1] and **culminating in** [Final Step].

**Specific Types (具体类型句型):**
*   **Life Cycle (生命周期):**
    > It is noticeable that the [Animal] passes through three distinct physical stages as it grows to maturity. Each of these stages takes place in a different environment.
*   **Manufacturing (生产流程):**
    > In general, the manufacturing process is a **complex/linear** system, encompassing both physical and chemical transformations from raw materials to the final product.

**Body Paragraphs (主体段):**
> **At the first stage,** [Step 1 happens]. **Subsequently/Following this,** [Step 2 happens].
> **During the final stage,** the product is [Final Action], ready for [Distribution/Consumption].

### 🧠 记忆口诀

> 🇨🇳 **delineate 开门见山描流程，数好阶段排成线，commence 开头 culminate 收尾，subsequently 一步一步往前赶。**
>
> 🇬🇧 *Delineate opens the door, count the stages, know the score, commence to start, culminate to end, subsequently step by step we wend.*
>
> Doccngad: delineates,overall,commencing,culminating,noticeable,general,at the first stage,during the final stage

## ⚖️ Task 2: Discuss Advantages & Disadvantages / 利弊分析类

**Introduction:**
> **In recent years/decades,** the issue of [Topic] has sparked considerable debate. While some view it as a positive development, others **are concerned about** its potential drawbacks. This essay will **scrutinize** both the benefits and distinct disadvantages of this trend.
> *（优化：替换了 "Nowadays... noticeable phenomenon"）*

**Body 1 (Advantages):**
> **On the one hand,** there are several compelling benefits to [Topic].
> **Primarily,** one significant advantage is that [Reason 1]. **For instance,** [Example].
> **Secondly,** another merit is that [Reason 2], which **facilitates** [Result].

**Body 2 (Disadvantages):**
> **On the other hand,** despite the upsides, there are potential pitfalls. One major concern is that [Drawback 1]. This may **result in** [Consequence].
> **Another potential downside** is [Drawback 2]. **In the absence of** proper regulation, this could lead to [Bad Outcome].

**Conclusion:**
> **In conclusion,** [Topic] presents both opportunities and challenges. While it offers [Key Benefit], it also carries risks such as [Key Risk]. **Therefore,** it is essential to weigh both sides carefully.

### 🧠 记忆口诀

> 🇨🇳 **左手拿着甜，右手拿着酸，on the one hand 两边摆，weigh both sides 称一称才算公正全面。**
>
> 🇬🇧 *One hand holds the gain, the other holds the pain, weigh them both with equal care, to make your argument fair.*
>
> Icopsoai:in recent years, concerned,on the other hand, primarily,secondly,on the other hand,another potential downside,in conslusion

## 🆚 Task 2: Agree or Disagree / 是否同意类

**Introduction:**
> The issue of [Topic] **has aroused intense discussion**. *(Or: The question of whether [Complete sentence, e.g., university education should be free] ...)* Some argue that [Opinion A], whereas others maintain that [Opinion B]. **From my perspective,** I firmly **agree/disagree** with the notion that [Your Stance].

**Body 1 (Main Reason 1):**
> **Firstly,** one of the primary reasons for my stance is that [Reason 1].
> *Explanation:* This means that [Elaboration].
> *Example:* **Take** [Example] **as a case in point**. This clearly demonstrates that [Closer].

**Body 2 (Main Reason 2):**
> **Furthermore,** another significant factor contributing to my view is [Reason 2].
> *Elaboration:* It is generally acknowledged that [Context]. **Therefore, it is evident that** [Point].

**Body 3 (Concession/Refutation - Optional but good for high scores):**
> **Admittedly,** opponents may argue that [Opposing View]. **To some extent,** this argument holds water(站得住脚). **However,** it overlooks the fact that [Counter-argument/Mitigation]. **Thus,** this does not undermine（侵蚀） my overall position.

**Conclusion:**

> **In conclusion,** although there are diverging views on [Topic], I remain convinced that [Restate Position]. The reasons outlined above, particularly [Reason 1] and [Reason 2], justify this viewpoint.

### 🧠 记忆口诀

> 🇨🇳 **perspective 先亮底牌，firstly 讲道理摆事实来，admittedly 退一步海阔天空，however 转身反杀 convinced 结尾不动摇。**
>
> 🇬🇧 *State your stance, don't be afraid, firstly reason, example laid, admittedly concede a bit, however counter — never quit, convinced at last, your case is made!*

## 🗣️ Task 2: Discuss Both Views / 双边讨论类

**Introduction:**
> **Contemporary society has witnessed** a heated debate over [Topic]. There are two primary schools(流派) of thought: some advocate [View A], while others champion [View B]. In this essay, I will discuss both perspectives and explain why I lean towards [Your View].

**Body 1 (View A):**
> **On the one hand,** proponents(拥护者)of [View A] present valid points. One of the main rationales(理由) is that [Reason 1]. **For example,** [Example], which indicates that [Result]. **Additionally,** [Reason 2].

**Body 2 (View B):**

> **On the other hand,** supporters of [View B] emphasize that [Reason 1]. **Specifically,** [Explain]. **A case in point is** [Example]. **Consequently,** [Result].

**Conclusion:**

> **To sum up,** both [View A] and [View B] have their own merits. **In my opinion,** a balanced approach (or specific side) is more ideal. We should [Suggestion/Final thought].

### 🧠 记忆口诀

> 🇨🇳 **witnessed 引来一场辩论赛，proponents / supporters 你方唱罢我登台，各讲各的好道理，sum up 收场我偏哪边说明白。**
>
> 🇬🇧 *Witnessed sparks the great debate, two sides speak — both advocate, sum it up and pick your lean, balanced view keeps your essay clean.*

## 📝 Task 2: Report (Problems/Causes + Solutions/Effects) / 报告类

**Introduction:**
> **It is becoming increasingly common to see** [Issue]. This essay will identify the underlying causes (or problems) of this phenomenon and propose some feasible solutions.

**Body 1 (Causes/Problems):**
> **There are several factors contributing to** this issue.
> **The most significant factor/problem is** [Cause 1]. This is largely due to [Explanation].
> **Another contributing factor is** [Cause 2]. **For example,** [Example].

**Body 2 (Solutions/Measures):**
> **To tackle this problem,** a number of measures can be adopted.
> **One effective solution is** that [Solution 1]. This would help to [Outcome].
> **In addition,** [Solution 2] should be implemented by [Government/Individuals]. If strictly enforced, this could **significantly alleviate** the situation.

**Conclusion:**
> **In conclusion,** [Issue] is mainly caused by [Reason 1] and [Reason 2]. To overcome it, joint efforts from [Who] and [Who] are required to [Action].

### 🧠 记忆口诀

> 🇨🇳 **increasingly 引出大问题，factors 刨根问底找原因，tackle 对症下药开方子，joint efforts 大家齐心来搞定。**
>
> 🇬🇧 *The problem grows for all to see, factors tell us why it'll be, tackle it with solutions clear, joint efforts make it disappear.*

## 🚀 Part 6: Advanced Sentence Structures & Vocabulary / 高分句型与词汇升级

这一部分整理了更加灵活、地道的表达方式，帮助你突破6.5/7分瓶颈。

### 6.1 Advanced Sentence Patterns (高分句型，情态，虚拟，强调，倒装)

1.  **Modal Verbs for Suggestions (情态动词表建议):**
    > *The government **should/could/might** + [Verb] + [Noun], which **can/could** [Result].*
    > *Example:* The government **should implement** stricter laws, which **can deter** criminals.

2.  **Subjunctive Mood (虚拟语气 - 假设情况):**
    > *If + [Subject] + **were to** [Verb], [Subject] + **would/could/might** + [Verb Base Form] / [be + Past Participle].*
    > *Example:* **If** the government **were to raise** fuel taxes, traffic congestion **could be significantly alleviated**.

3.  **Cleft Sentences (强调句 - 强调主语/原因等):**
    > *It is + [Emphasized Part] + **that** + [Rest of Sentence].*
    > *Example:* **It is** the lack of education **that** leads to poverty.

4.  **Inversion (倒装句 - 强调唯一方法):**
    > *Only by [doing sth] **can** + [Subject] + [Verb].*
    > *Example:* **Only by cooperating globally can** we solve climate change.

### 🧠 高分句型记忆口诀

> 🇨🇳 **should 提建议，were to 做假设，It is that 拎重点，Only by 翻过来写——四招亮出来，作文分数自然高上来。**
>
> 🇬🇧 *Should suggests, were to pretends, It is...that emphasis lends, Only by inverts with might — four tricks make your writing bright!*

### 6.2 Flexible Vocabulary for Trends (图表题词汇升级)

#### Noun Expressions (名词表达)
*   **Experience/Witness/Show/See**:
    > *Example:* The year 2020 **witnessed/saw** a slight increase in sales.

#### Verb Expressions (动词表达 - 替换 increase/decrease)
*   **Upward (上升):**
    *   **Inch up / Tick up / Edge up**: 微微上涨 (Small increase).
    *   **Grow / Climb / Rise**: 一般上升.
    *   **Soar / Surge / Skyrocket**: 飞涨/急升 (Large + Fast).

*   **Downward (下降):**
    *   **Dip**: 微微下降 (Small drop).
    *   **Drop / Fall / Decline / Go down**: 一般下降.
    *   **Plunge / Plummet / Slump**: 骤降/暴跌 (Large + Fast).

*   **No Change (不变):**
    *   **Remain stable / steady / unchanged**.
    *   **Level off at**.

*   **Fluctuation (波动):**
    *   **Rose sharply to X, but then declined to Y before recovering to Z.** (先升后降再升)
    *   **Increased to X, dipped, and then rose to Y.** (升 -> 跌 -> 升)

#### Comparison & Degree (对比与程度)
*   **Overtake (超过):**
    > *The year 2013 marked the point at which the percentage of A **overtook** that for B.*

*   **Adjectives for Degree (形容词):**
    *   **Large:** Significant, remarkable, sharp, noticeable, steep, dramatic, considerable, marked.
    *   **Small:** Modest, slight, marginal.

### 🧠 图表趋势词汇口诀

> 🇨🇳 **inch 悄悄爬，climb 慢慢走，soar 一飞冲天到云端；plunge 跳崖摔下来，stable 纹丝不动如泰山，dip 一下又弹回来那叫波动玩得欢。**
>
> 🇬🇧 *Inch up slow, climb with grace, soar to the sky at rapid pace; plunge and plummet, falling fast, stable steady, standing last; dip and bounce — fluctuations dance, each word chosen gives your chart a chance!*

### 6.3 Critical Thinking Questions (拓展思路)

在写作时，可以问自己以下问题来加深论证：
*   **Competitiveness**: How does it affect individuals? (e.g., in career, education)
*   **Evaluation**: Is it a positive or negative trend?
