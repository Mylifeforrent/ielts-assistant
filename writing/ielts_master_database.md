## 🏛️ Topic 1: Government / 政府类

### 💡 Idea Bank (素材清单)

#### 🟢 Benefits & Responsibilities (职责与好处)
*   **Undertake social responsibilities**: 承担社会责任.
*   **Invest in infrastructure**: 投资基础设施 (Schools, Roads).
*   **Satisfy spiritual and cultural needs**: 满足精神文化需求. 
*   **Ensure national security**: 保障国家安全.
*   **Boost economy and education**: 促进经济和教育发展.
*   **Maintain social stability**: 维护社会稳定.
> **🧠 记忆口诀 (职责偏)**: ***稳全财责基文***
>
> **“稳(Stability，社会稳定) 全(Security，安全) 财(Economy，经济+教育) 责(Responsibility，社会责任)基(Infrastructure，基建)文(Culture，文化)”** —— 政府要稳住政权，搞好经济，并在基建文化上尽责。

#### 🔴 Problems (政府面临的问题)
*   **Endure financial burden**: 承受财政负担.
*   **High rate of corruption**: 高腐败率.
*   **Lack of effective censorship**: 缺乏有效监管.
*   **Impose political intention**: 强加政治意图.
> **🧠 记忆口诀 (坏处篇)**: ***没钱坏人强行政治乱管***
>
> **“没钱(Burden) 腐败的坏人(Corruption) 强行政治乱管(impose intention，Censorship)”**。

#### 🔧 Measures (解决措施)
*   **Levy taxes & Issue national bonds**: 征税与发行国债.
*   **Enact legislation and regulations**: 制定法律法规.
*   **Improve moral standards**: 提高道德标准.
*   **Broaden channels to raise funds**: 拓宽融资渠道.
> **🧠 记忆口诀 (措施篇)**: ***融资征税立规教化***
>
> **“融资征税(Funds，Tax) 立规(Laws) 教化(Moral)”**。

### 🔗 Logic Flows (分步论证)

#### 1. Public Services (为什么要投资公共服务?)
> **Logic**: Impose taxes (征税) -> Undertake responsibilities (担责) -> Invest in infrastructure (基建) -> Enhance public welfare (福利) -> Foster social stability (稳定).

#### 2. Arts vs. Essential Services (艺术 vs 民生)
> **Pro-Arts**: Arts represent culture (代表文化) -> Preserve heritage (遗产) -> Strengthen national identity (认同感).
> **Anti-Arts**: Funds are limited (资金有限) -> Prioritize urgent needs (医疗教育优先) -> Arts are merely personal entertainment (个人娱乐).

## 🌍 Topic 2: Environment / 环境类

### 💡 Idea Bank (素材清单)

#### 🟢 Benefits of Protection (环保好处)
*   **Reduce energy consumption**: 减少能源消耗.
*   **Foster sustainable development**: 促进可持续发展.
*   **Develop renewable energy**: 开发可再生能源.
*   **Safeguard biodiversity**: 保护生物多样性.
*   **Conserve natural resources**: 节约自然资源.
> **🧠 记忆口诀**:***节流开源可持续多样性***
>
>  **“节流(Conserve,自然资源，能源) 开源(Renewable，可再生) 可持续(sustainable） 多样性(Biodiversity，生物多样性)”**。

#### 🔴 Environmental Hazards (环境危害)
*   **Exacerbate environmental deterioration**: 加剧环境恶化.
*   **Climate change intensification (Global warming)**: 气候变化加剧.
*   **Endanger biodiversity**: 威胁生物多样性.
*   **Lead to desertification and soil erosion**: 导致荒漠化和水土流失.
*   **Drain energy resources**: 耗尽能源资源.
> **🧠 记忆口诀**: ***环境气候沙漠耗能威生***
>
> **“3D危机，环境气候沙漠耗尽能源威胁生物多样性”**: **Deterioration** (恶化), **Desertification** (沙漠化), **Drain** (耗尽).

### 🔗 Logic Flows (分步论证)

#### 1. The Problem Chain (问题链)
> **Logic**: Rapid industrialization (工业化) -> Massive greenhouse gas emissions (排放) -> Greenhouse effect (温室效应) -> Global warming (变暖) -> Extreme weather events (极端天气).

#### 2. The Solution Chain (解决链)
> **Logic**: Enact strict laws (立法) + Develop clean energy (清洁能源) -> Reduce carbon footprint (碳足迹) -> Mitigate environmental damage (减轻破坏).

## 🎓 Topic 3: Education / 教育类

### 💡 Idea Bank (素材清单)

#### 🟢 Goals of Education (教育目标)
*   **Improve moral standards**: 提升道德修养.
*   **Cultivate critical thinking skills**: 培养批判性思维.
*   **Nurture comprehensive skills**: 培养综合能力.
*   **Meet industry demands**: 满足行业需求.
*   **Improve career prospects**: 提升就业前景.
> **🧠 记忆口诀**: 思维修养有能力，行业用人有前景
>
> 思维(critical thinking)修养(moral standards)有能力(comprehensive skills)，行业(industry)用人有前景（prospects）
>
> **“脑(Thinking) + 手(Skills/Industry) + 心(Moral)”** —— 也就是德智体美劳。

#### 🔴 Education Issues (教育问题)
*   **Exam-oriented education (Cramming)**: 应试教育/填鸭式教育.
*   **Stifle creativity and innovation**: 扼杀创造力和创新.
*   **Lack of social practice**: 缺乏社会实践.
*   **Limit personal thought**: 限制个人思想.
*   **Impose financial pressure on families**: 给家庭带来经济压力.
> **🧠 记忆口诀**: **“死读书(Cramming/No practice) + 费钱(Financial) + 变傻(Stifle creativity，personal thoughts)”**。

### 🔗 Logic Flows (分步论证)

#### 1. University Education (大学)
> **Logic**: Theoretical knowledge (理论) -> Systematic learning (系统学习) -> Adaptability (适应力) -> Decent salary (高薪) -> Promising career prospects (前景).

#### 2. Vocational Training (技校)
> **Logic**: Practical skills (实操) -> Specific job training (针对性) -> Meet market demand (需求) -> Quick employment (快速就业).

## 📱 Topic 4: Technology & Media / 科技媒体

### 💡 Idea Bank (素材清单)

#### 🟢 Tech Benefits & Media Pros (好处)
*   **Offer great convenience**: 提供极大便利.
*   **Enhance working efficiency**: 提高工作效率.
*   **Promote cross-regional exchanges**: 促进跨地域交流.
*   **Maintain the public's right to know**: 维护公众知情权 (Media).
*   **Expose social issues**: 曝光社会问题 (Media).
> **🧠 记忆口诀**: **“快(Efficiency) 广(Exchange) 真(Right to know)曝(social issues)利(convenience)”**。

#### 🔴 Tech Harms & Media Cons (坏处)
*   **Result in unemployment (Automation)**: 导致失业 (自动化).
*   **Invasion of privacy**: 侵犯隐私.
*   **Cyber fraud and crimes**: 网络诈骗.
*   **Fabricate and exaggerate facts**: 捏造和夸大事实.
*   **Trigger social panic**: 引发社会恐慌.
*   **Misleading the public**: 误导大众.
> **🧠 记忆口诀**: **“懒宅惨(Tech，太懒容易被侵犯privacy，automation导致失业)” + “假大空(Media，诈骗，大众，夸大，恐慌)”** —— 科技让人变懒变宅，媒体搞假新闻吓人。

### 🔗 Logic Flows (分步论证)

#### 1. Isolation (科技导致的疏远)
> **Logic**: Addiction to screens (沉迷屏幕) -> Less face-to-face interaction (见面少) -> Rely on virtual communication (虚拟) -> Erode social skills (社恐) -> Social alienation (疏远).

## 👥 Topic 5: Society & Crime / 社会与犯罪

### 💡 Idea Bank (素材清单)

#### 🟢 Ideal Society (理想社会)
*   **Narrow the gap between rich and poor**: 缩小贫富差距.
*   **Protect vulnerable groups**: 保护弱势群体 (Elderly, disabled).
*   **Promote social fairness and justice**: 促进公平正义.
*   **Decrease the crime rate**: 降低犯罪率.
*   **Enhance social cohesion**: 增强社会凝聚力.
> **🧠 记忆口诀**: **“扶弱(Vulnerable，弱势群体，犯罪) 均富(Gap，贫富差距，公平) 团结(Cohesion)”**。

#### 🔴 Social Problems & Crime (问题)
*   **Widening wealth gap**: 贫富差距扩大.
*   **Social class inequality**: 社会阶级不平等.
*   **Aging population**: 人口老龄化.
*   **High recidivism rate**: 高再犯率.
*   **Impede fair competition**: 阻碍公平竞争.
> **🧠 记忆口诀**: **“不公(Inequality,贫富差距，公平竞争) 变老(Aging，老龄化) 难民(Refugees 社会阶层不平等，犯罪率)”**。

### 🔗 Logic Flows (分步论证)

#### 1. Prison vs. Rehabilitation (监狱 vs 改造)
> **Prison**: Severe punishment (严惩) -> Act as a deterrent (震慑作用) -> Ensure public safety (安全).
> **Rehab**: Education & Job training (教育培训) -> Acquire survival skills (生存技能) -> Re-integrate into society (重返社会) -> Reduce recidivism (降低再犯).

## 💰 Topic 6: Economy & Money / 经济与金钱

### 💡 Idea Bank (素材清单)

#### 🟢 Economic Prosperity (繁荣)
*   **Stimulate economic development**: 刺激经济发展.
*   **Create abundant job opportunities**: 创造大量就业机会.
*   **Enhance social productivity**: 提高社会生产力.
*   **Lift people out of poverty**: 帮助脱贫.
*   **Improve the quality of life**: 提高生活质量.
> **🧠 记忆口诀**: **“钱多(Economy，经济发展) 活多(Jobs，就业机会，生产力) 生活好(Quality，脱贫，生活质量)”**。

#### 🔴 Economic Recession (衰退)
*   **Economic recession / Downturn**: 经济衰退.
*   **Increase economic burden**: 增加经济负担.
*   **Lead to poverty and inequality**: 导致贫困和不平等.
*   **Degrade living standards**: 生活水平下降.
*   **Unemployment rate soaring**: 失业率飙升.
> **🧠 记忆口诀**: **“钱少(经济衰退，经济负担) 活少(Unemployment，失业) 生活差(Degrade standards，贫困，生活水平下降)”**。

### 🔗 Logic Flows (分步论证)

#### 1. Economic Growth vs Environmental Protection (发展经济 vs 保护环境)
> **Logic**: Prioritize economic growth (优先发展经济) -> Industrial expansion (工业扩张) -> Over-exploitation of resources (过度开采资源) -> Environmental degradation (导致环境恶化) -> Threaten sustainable development (威胁可持续发展).

### 💡 Idea Bank (素材清单)
