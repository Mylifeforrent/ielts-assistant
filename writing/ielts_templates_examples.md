# IELTS Writing Templates — 实战范文集 ✍️

本文档对应 `ielts_templates_compiled.md` 中的每一类模版，各提供 **至少一篇完整范文**，展示模版的实际用法。

> 💡 **使用方法**: 对照模版文件，观察每个 **加粗** 的短语如何被填充为具体内容。模版的"骨架"不变，你只需替换 `[括号]` 中的内容。

## 🗺️ Example 1: Task 1 — Maps / 地图题

**题目:** *The two maps below show the village of Ryemouth in 1995 and 2005. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.*

---

### 范文

The two maps **illustrate** the developments that have taken place in a particular place called **Ryemouth** over a ten-year period from **1995** to **2005**.

**Overall, it is evident that** the area has become more **urbanized** than before. The most noticeable changes are the construction of new infrastructure and amenities, often at the expense of green areas.

**To begin with,** in **1995**, **a large area of farmland** was located in the **north of the village**. **However,** it was replaced by **a new housing estate** to make way for **accommodating the growing population**.

**Furthermore**, the **village centre** has been **enlarged** by adding **a shopping mall and a car park**, which drastically reduced the size of **the original park and playground**.

**Finally,** it is worth noting(值得注意的是 注意是这里是noting, 可以理解为notice的动词形式) that only **the river running through the southern edge** and **the old church on the hill** have remained **largely unchanged** over the period, although **the church** was slightly renovated.

> 📝 **技巧解析:**
> - "illustrate" 替代了 "show"，更学术
> - "at the expense of green areas" 是高分表达，意为"以绿地为代价"
> - 注意方位词的灵活使用：north, southern edge, on the hill
> - **illustrate 开场白，overall 说大改，这拆那建讲变化，unchanged 结尾最自在。**

## ⚙️ Example 2: Task 1 — Process / 流程图

**题目:** *The diagram below shows the process of making olive oil. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features.*

---

### 范文

The diagram **delineates** the process of producing **olive oil**.

**Overall,** the process consists of **five** distinct stages, **commencing with** **the harvesting of olives** and **culminating in** **the packaging of the final product**.

**In general,** the manufacturing process is a **linear** system, encompassing both physical and chemical transformations from raw materials to the final product.

**At the first stage,** **ripe olives are picked from trees, either by hand or using machines**. **Subsequently,** **the harvested olives are washed and any leaves or stems are removed**.

**Following this,** **the clean olives are crushed into a paste using a stone mill or modern press**. **The paste is then placed into a centrifuge, which separates the oil from the water and solid residue.**

**During the final stage,** the product is **filtered and bottled**, ready for **distribution to supermarkets and restaurants**.

> 📝 **技巧解析:**
> - "delineates" 比 "shows" 更高级，意为"描绘/勾勒"
> - "commencing with... culminating in..." 是描述流程起止的经典搭配
> - 被动语态 (are picked, are washed, are crushed) 是流程图描述的标配
> - **delineate 开门见山描流程，数好阶段排成线，commence 开头 culminate 收尾，subsequently 一步一步往前赶。**

## ⚖️ Example 3: Task 2 — Advantages & Disadvantages / 利弊分析类

**题目:** *More and more people are working from home rather than in an office. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of this trend.*

---

### 范文

**In recent years,** the issue of **remote working** has sparked considerable debate. While some view it as a positive development, others **are concerned about** its potential drawbacks. This essay will **scrutinize** both the benefits and distinct disadvantages of this trend.

**On the one hand,** there are several compelling benefits to **working from home**. **Primarily,** one significant advantage is that **employees can save a considerable amount of time and money by eliminating the daily commute**. **For instance,** **a worker who previously spent two hours commuting each day could redirect that time to exercise, family, or professional development**. **Secondly,** another merit is that **remote work provides greater flexibility in managing one's schedule**, which **facilitates** **a healthier work-life balance**.

**On the other hand,** despite the upsides, there are potential pitfalls. One major concern is that **prolonged isolation from colleagues may lead to feelings of loneliness and disconnection**. This may **result in** **decreased motivation and, ultimately, lower productivity**. **Another potential downside** is **the blurring of boundaries between personal and professional life**. **In the absence of** proper regulation, this could lead to **chronic overwork and burnout**.

**In conclusion,** **remote working** presents both opportunities and challenges. While it offers **significant savings in time and improved flexibility**, it also carries risks such as **social isolation and work-life imbalance**. **Therefore,** it is essential to weigh both sides carefully.

> 📝 **技巧解析:**
> - "scrutinize" 表示"仔细审视"，比 "discuss" 更有深度
> - "In the absence of proper regulation" 是高分条件表达
> - 结论段简洁回顾双方观点，不引入新论点

## 🆚 Example 4: Task 2 — Agree or Disagree / 是否同意类

**题目:** *Some people believe that university education should be free for all students. To what extent do you agree or disagree?*

---

### 范文

The issue of **whether university education should be free** **has aroused intense discussion**. Some argue that **higher education is a fundamental right and should be funded by the government**, whereas others maintain that **students should bear the cost as they are the primary beneficiaries**. **From my perspective,** I firmly **agree** with the notion that **tuition fees should be abolished**.

**Firstly,** one of the primary reasons for my stance is that **free education promotes equal opportunities regardless of socioeconomic background**. *Explanation:* This means that **talented students from low-income families would no longer be deterred from pursuing higher education due to financial barriers**. *Example:* **Take** **the Nordic countries such as Finland and Norway** **as a case in point** — **they offer tuition-free university education, and consequently rank among the highest in the world for social mobility and innovation**. This clearly demonstrates that **removing financial barriers yields broad societal benefits**.

**Furthermore,** another significant factor contributing to my view is **the long-term economic return of an educated workforce**. *Elaboration:* It is generally acknowledged that **graduates tend to earn higher salaries and pay more in taxes over their lifetimes**. **Therefore, it is evident that** **the government's investment in free education would be repaid many times over through increased tax revenue and reduced welfare costs**.

**Admittedly,** opponents may argue that **free education would place an enormous burden on taxpayers**. **To some extent,** this argument holds water. **However,** it overlooks the fact that **many governments already allocate substantial budgets to areas of less direct benefit, and redirecting even a portion of military or administrative spending could fund higher education sustainably**. **Thus,** this does not undermine my overall position.

**In conclusion,** although there are diverging views on **the funding of university education**, I remain convinced that **it should be made free for all students**. The reasons outlined above, particularly **the promotion of social equality** and **the long-term economic returns**, justify this viewpoint.

> 📝 **技巧解析:**
> - 开头段清晰表明立场："I firmly agree"
> - Body 3 的让步段 (Admittedly...) 展现批判性思维，是冲 7+ 的关键
> - "holds water" 是地道表达，意为"站得住脚"
> - 结论段不引入新论点，只总结前文核心理由

## 🗣️ Example 5: Task 2 — Discuss Both Views / 双边讨论类

**题目:** *Some people think that all teenagers should be required to do unpaid community work in their free time. Others believe this would be an interference with their personal freedom. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.*

---

### 范文

**Contemporary society has witnessed** a heated debate over **whether teenagers should be obligated to participate in unpaid community service**. There are two primary schools of thought: some advocate **mandatory community work for all young people**, while others champion **the right of teenagers to decide how they spend their free time**. In this essay, I will discuss both perspectives and explain why I lean towards **a balanced, voluntary approach**.

**On the one hand,** proponents of **compulsory community service** present valid points. One of the main rationales is that **it instils a sense of civic responsibility and empathy in young people at a formative age**. **For example,** **a teenager volunteering at a local food bank would gain first-hand understanding of poverty and inequality**, which indicates that **such experiences can shape more compassionate and socially aware adults**. **Additionally,** **community work helps teenagers develop practical skills — like teamwork, time management, and communication — that are not always taught in schools**.

**On the other hand,** supporters of **personal freedom** emphasize that **forcing unpaid labour on young people could breed resentment rather than genuine altruism**. **Specifically,** **if a teenager is compelled to volunteer against their will, they are unlikely to engage meaningfully with the work, reducing its value for both the volunteer and the community**. **A case in point is** **the mandatory community service programme in certain American high schools, where studies have shown that students often treat the requirement as a checkbox exercise rather than a learning opportunity**. **Consequently,** **such policies may fail to achieve their intended purpose**.

**To sum up,** both **mandatory community work** and **personal freedom** have their own merits. **In my opinion,** a balanced approach is more ideal. We should **encourage teenagers to volunteer through incentives — such as academic credit or certificates — rather than impose it as an obligation, thereby fostering genuine engagement and preserving individual autonomy**.

> 📝 **技巧解析:**
> - "two primary schools of thought" 比 "two different opinions" 更学术
> - Body 中引用了具体案例（American high schools），增强说服力
> - 结论给出折中建议，展现思维深度

## 📝 Example 6: Task 2 — Report / 报告类 (原因 + 解决方案)

**题目:** *In many cities, the number of people cycling to work is declining. What are the causes of this problem and what solutions can be proposed?*

---

### 范文

**It is becoming increasingly common to see** **a decline in the number of urban commuters who choose to cycle to work**. This essay will identify the underlying causes of this phenomenon and propose some feasible solutions.

**There are several factors contributing to** this issue. **The most significant factor is** **the lack of adequate cycling infrastructure in most cities**. This is largely due to **urban planning that has historically prioritised cars over bicycles, resulting in a shortage of dedicated bike lanes and secure parking facilities**. **Another contributing factor is** **the growing perception that cycling is unsafe due to heavy traffic and frequent accidents involving cyclists**. **For example,** **a recent survey in London revealed that over 60% of former cyclists stopped riding due to safety concerns on busy roads**.

**To tackle this problem,** a number of measures can be adopted. **One effective solution is** that **city governments invest in expanding and improving cycling infrastructure, including protected bike lanes, traffic-calming measures, and bike-sharing stations**. This would help to **make cycling a more convenient and appealing option for daily commuters**. **In addition,** **public awareness campaigns highlighting the health and environmental benefits of cycling** should be implemented by **local authorities and health organisations**. If strictly enforced, this could **significantly alleviate** the situation.

**In conclusion,** **the decline in urban cycling** is mainly caused by **inadequate infrastructure** and **safety concerns**. To overcome it, joint efforts from **governments** and **public health organisations** are required to **create safer cycling environments and shift public attitudes in favour of this sustainable mode of transport**.

> 📝 **技巧解析:**
> - 开头段避免了 "Nowadays... phenomenon" 的模版句
> - Body 1 中因果逻辑清晰: factor → due to → resulting in
> - "If strictly enforced" 增加了条件层次
> - 结论段干净利落：原因 + 解决方案 + 行动号召

## 🎯 总结：模版使用要点

| 步骤 | 要点 |
|:---|:---|
| **1. 识别题型** | 先判断是 Task 1 还是 Task 2，再细分具体类型 |
| **2. 套用骨架** | 用模版的连接词和结构搭好框架 |
| **3. 填充内容** | 将具体题目的信息填入 `[括号]` 位置 |
| **4. 润色语言** | 使用 Part 6 中的高分句型和词汇替换基础表达 |
| **5. 检查逻辑** | 确保每段有 Topic Sentence → Explanation → Example |

> ⚠️ **切记**: 模版是工具，不是目的。考官看重的是你的 **逻辑思维** 和 **语言灵活性**，而非背诵能力。请在练习中逐步内化这些结构，用自己的话表达出来。
